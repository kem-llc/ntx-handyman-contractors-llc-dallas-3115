Common questions

What materials do you recommend for Dallas weather?

For decks, we recommend composite or pressure-treated pine with stainless steel fasteners to withstand humidity and heat. For fences, cedar or vinyl are excellent choices.

Do you handle permits?

Yes, we obtain all necessary permits and ensure your deck or fence meets local building codes.
