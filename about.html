Our story

Dedicated to Quality, Committed to Dallas

To provide exceptional handyman services that improve the comfort, safety, and beauty of our clients' homes and businesses, while building lasting relationships based on trust and integrity.

NTX Handyman & Contractors LLC was founded with a simple mission: deliver high-quality home repair and renovation services with honesty and professionalism. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of skilled craftsmen serving the Dallas area. Our founder, a lifelong Dallas resident, saw a need for reliable handymen who show up on time, communicate clearly, and take pride in their work.

Over the years, we have completed thousands of projects, from minor fixes to full-scale renovations. Our reputation is built on word-of-mouth referrals and a commitment to exceeding expectations. We treat every home like our own and every client like family. Today, NTX Handyman is a go-to resource for homeowners and businesses who value quality, reliability, and fair pricing.
